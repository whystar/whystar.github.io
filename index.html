Zhixing Li (李志星)

Hi there! I am currently a M.S. candidate of Computer Science at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). I joined Trustie Group at August 2015, advised by associate Prof. Gang Yin. Before that, I got my B.E. degree from Chongqing University (CQU) in 2015.

My main research interest is software engineering, spanning from mining software repositories and open source communities, analyzing social code reviews and software crowdsourcing.
