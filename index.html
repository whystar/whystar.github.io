Yue Yu (余跃)

Hi there! I am currently an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at National University of Defense Technology. I joined Trustie Group at August 2011, advised by Prof. Huaimin Wang. Fortunately, I have got the great opportunity to visit UC Davis supported by CSC scholarship.

My main research interest is software engineering, spanning from mining software repositories, analyzing social coding networks and software crowdsourcing.
